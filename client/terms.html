2. How RPS Arena Works

2.1 Game Overview

RPS Arena is a skill-based competitive game where three players compete in real-time. Each player is assigned a role (Rock, Paper, or Scissors) and must use movement strategy to eliminate opponents or collect objectives to win.

2.2 Entry Fee and Prize Pool

  • Entry Fee: 1 USDC per match
  • Total Prize Pool: 3 USDC (from 3 players)
  • Winner Payout: 2.4 USDC
  • Platform Fee: 0.6 USDC (20% of prize pool)

2.3 Match Rules

  1. Three players join a lobby, each paying a 1 USDC entry fee
  2. Each player is randomly assigned a role: Rock, Paper, or Scissors
  3. Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock
  4. Players move around the arena and collide with opponents to eliminate them
  5. If two players remain, the game enters "Showdown Mode" where players race to collect hearts
  6. The last player standing (or first to collect 2 hearts in Showdown) wins

2.4 Skill-Based Competition

RPS Arena is a game of skill. While initial role assignment is random, the outcome depends on player movement, positioning, strategy, and reaction time. Success requires understanding game mechanics, anticipating opponent movements, and making strategic decisions in real-time.
